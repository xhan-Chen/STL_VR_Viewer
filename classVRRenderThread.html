Detailed Description

Runs the VTK/OpenVR scene on a worker thread.

The desktop renderer and the VR renderer cannot safely share live VTK pipeline objects while filters are being changed. This class therefore receives actor and geometry snapshots before or during VR rendering, applies queued changes inside run(), and emits desktop-space transform/colour updates back to MainWindow.
